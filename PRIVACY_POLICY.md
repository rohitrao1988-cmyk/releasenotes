## Overview

DocScan(free) ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application **DocScan(free)** (the "Application").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Documents and Files:**
- Any documents you scan using the Application
- Photos and images you import
- Text you add via signatures or stamps
- File metadata (name, date created, etc.)

**All document data is stored exclusively on your device.**

### 1.2 Information We DO NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect, transmit, or store:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- App usage analytics
- Browsing history
- Financial information
- Health information
- Behavioral tracking data

### 1.3 Automatic Information

The Application operates entirely offline. We do **NOT** collect:
- IP addresses
- Device type or operating system version
- App crash reports
- Performance analytics
- User session data

---

## 2. How We Use Information

### 2.1 Use of Information

Your documents are used **ONLY** for:
- Displaying your scanned documents in the Application
- Processing scanning, editing, and PDF generation
- Creating backups you manually initiate
- Sharing files you explicitly request

### 2.2 We Do NOT Use Information For:

- Marketing or advertising
- Third-party profiling
- Behavioral analysis
- Data selling or sharing
- Targeted advertising
- Machine learning training

---

## 3. Information Sharing

### 3.1 No Third-Party Sharing

We **DO NOT** share your documents or personal information with:
- Third-party services
- Advertising networks
- Analytics providers
- Social media platforms
- Cloud storage services
- Any external entities

### 3.2 Explicit User Sharing

You have full control over sharing. Your documents are only shared when you:
- Explicitly select "Share" in the Application
- Choose a destination (Email, WhatsApp, Drive, etc.)
- Confirm the share action

The Application never shares data automatically or without your explicit consent.

---

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Encryption

- All documents are encrypted using **AES-256 encryption**
- Military-grade security standard
- Encryption occurs on your device only
- Encryption keys never leave your device

### 4.2 On-Device Storage

- 100% of your data remains on your device
- No cloud synchronization
- No server backups
- No network transmission of documents
- No data collection servers

### 4.3 Security Measures

- No internet connectivity required
- No registration or authentication systems
- No login credentials stored
- No remote access capabilities

## 10. Permissions

### 10.1 Required Permissions

The Application requests only necessary permissions:

**Android Permissions:**
- `CAMERA` - To scan documents with device camera
- `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` - To import images/files
- `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` - To save scanned documents
- `INTERNET` - For shared preferences (local only, no data transmitted)

**iOS Permissions:**
- `PHOTO_LIBRARY` - To access photos for importing
- `CAMERA` - To scan documents

### 10.2 Permission Use

Permissions are used **only** for:
- Capturing camera images (yours to control)
- Accessing files you select (your choice)
- Saving documents to device storage
- No background monitoring
- No location tracking
